# Submission Checklist — Release Pilot v1.0

Final pre-flight before you tap **Submit for Review** in App Store Connect.

Print this. Cross off each line as you do it. Do not skip — the items are ordered by what gets you rejected first.

## 🚨 Block-the-launch issues (do these or you will be rejected)

- [ ] **Fix `MISSING_METADATA` on both IAPs** in App Store Connect → Subscriptions
  - Each product needs: localized display name, localized description, review screenshot, review note
  - Both products must be in the **same Subscription Group** (e.g. `release_pilot_pro`)
  - Both must show status **"Ready to Submit"** before you ship
  - This was already flagged in a RevenueCat warning earlier — handle it FIRST
- [ ] **Privacy Policy URL is hosted and works** at `https://releasepilot.app/privacy`
  - Use `app-store/PRIVACY_POLICY.md`, convert to HTML, host on GitHub Pages or Cloudflare Pages
  - **Test the link from a fresh browser session** — Apple will check it
  - Fill in the `[FILL IN]` placeholders (effective date, jurisdiction, postal address)
- [ ] **Support URL is hosted and works** at `https://releasepilot.app/support`
  - Minimum: a page with your email and an "Email me" button
  - Apple will reject if this is a `mailto:` URL in the listing field — must be `https://`
- [ ] **Account deletion path is functional**
  - Verify: open app → More → ASC Accounts → swipe-delete → all data gone
  - Apple has rejected apps that lack this since 2022 (Guideline 5.1.1(v))
  - Optional but recommended: add a "Delete all data" button somewhere obvious
- [ ] **Production APNs (not sandbox) is wired up and verified end-to-end**
  - `app.json` already has `aps-environment: production` ✅
  - Verify your Cloudflare Worker uses the **production** APNs endpoint, not sandbox
  - Send a test push to a production build and watch it arrive

## 🔵 Day-of-launch ops

- [ ] **Have a way to reach users post-submit**
  - At minimum: working email at `senthil930@gmail.com`
  - Optional: a Twitter/X account or Bluesky handle for the app
- [ ] **Have a way to monitor crashes**
  - At v1.0: read the App Store Connect → Crashes view daily for the first week
  - At v1.1: add Sentry or Bugsnag (~30 min of setup work)
- [ ] **Decide TestFlight beta tester list**
  - Even 3-5 trusted indie devs will catch issues App Review won't
  - Test for 3-7 days BEFORE submitting to App Review
- [ ] **Pre-write a "launch day" tweet/post**
  - Don't write it after Apple approves — your hands will shake. Write it now, queue it later.
- [ ] **Pre-write a "review reply" for the first 1-star review**
  - The first 1-star is always shaped like "doesn't work" with no detail
  - Have a template ready: "Sorry it's not working for you! Can you email me at senthil930@gmail.com with what device + iOS version you're on? I'll get on it today."

## 🟣 After you tap Submit (the next 48 hours)

- [ ] **Apple's review SLA is 24-48 hours** for most submissions in 2026
- [ ] **If rejected**: read the rejection carefully, reply to App Review in App Store Connect's Resolution Center (NOT by re-submitting blindly), turn it around within 24h
- [ ] **If "In Review"** for > 72 hours: that's normal for first-time submissions of subscription apps — sit tight
- [ ] **Once approved**:
  - Don't auto-release. Use **manual release** so you can pick the launch hour
  - Best launch time: **Tuesday or Wednesday at 10 AM Pacific** (highest organic traffic, full week to capitalize on reviews)
  - Activate the IAPs in ASC ONLY when the app is approved (otherwise the IAPs may be in "Waiting for Review" while the app is "Ready for Sale")
