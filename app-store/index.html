The pain

Submit. Refresh. Refresh. Refresh.

Every solo dev knows the loop: submit at 11pm, then refresh App Store Connect every 4 hours for the next 2 days waiting for "In Review." Multiply by 4 apps. Multiply by 5 review responses. Multiply by 12 release cycles a year.

The fix

One screen. Every app. Live pushes.

Release Pilot is the App Store Connect companion built for solo iOS developers who ship more than one app. Live multi-app status, push notifications, Lock Screen widgets, pre-submit checklist, and review replies — all from your phone.

What's inside

Everything you need to ship calmly

Six things that make the Apple-provided App Store Connect app feel like the bare minimum.

📡

Multi-app dashboard

See the live status of every app at a glance. Drafting, Submitted, In Review, Approved, Live — color-coded, pull-to-refresh, no scraping.

🔔

Push the moment Apple moves

Get an iOS push the second your app transitions states — including rejections with the review note inline so you can start fixing immediately.

📱

Lock Screen widget

Surfaces whichever app most needs your attention right now. Six widget sizes including circular and inline for the Lock Screen.

⏱️

Live Activity during review

Watch your "Waiting for Review" countdown right in the Dynamic Island. No more unlocking the phone to peek.

✅

Pre-submit checklist

Catches the mechanical things that get apps rejected: missing description, keyword overflow, no support URL, blank IAP metadata, version draft still open.

💬

Review replies + RevenueCat

Reply to App Store reviews from your phone with reusable templates. See live MRR and revenue on the Today tab via your RevenueCat project.
